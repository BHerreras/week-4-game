Gems On JQuery

Instructions:

You will be shown a random number at the start of the game.

By clicking on a gem, you will add a specific amount of points to your Current Total.

The goal is to match your Current Total to the Random Number. You will lose if you go over.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
